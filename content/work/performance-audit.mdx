<Chapter title="Problem">

A product-listing page was showing LCP of 4.8 s on mobile. Cart conversion was 12% below target.

</Chapter>

<Chapter title="Diagnosis">

Waterfall analysis revealed a render-blocking third-party script, unoptimized hero images, and a missing edge-cache layer on the product API.

</Chapter>

<Chapter title="Resolution">

Deferred the third-party script, switched hero images to next/image with priority, and added a Vercel Edge Config cache for product data.

LCP dropped to 1.9 s. Conversion recovered to target within two weeks.

</Chapter>
